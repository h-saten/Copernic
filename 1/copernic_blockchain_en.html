As part of the Copernic project, we give the broader audience the opportunity to participate in the renewable energy sources boom. We achieve this goal by combining the photovoltaic business with the blockchain industry. We offer our investors an opportunity to become a micro-producer of renewable energy without the need to install any system on their house’s roof. By purchasing COP tokens, they support us building a photovoltaic farm with 1 MW (or one million watts) output power.

Previous issues

The first project, COP1, resulted in a success confirmed by 413 investors and 40 strategic partners. We raised precisely 821.472,27 PLN, which was almost 70% of the assumed objective for the construction of the first farm in Stary Dzików.

The success of the first fundraising campaign shows that investors put trust in the project, which is why the second COP2 project started soon. With the support of 460 investors, we collected precisely 916.270,34 PLN, which was over 76% of our objective. But that is not all, so it’s time to build more farms. Together, we’ll build a thousand!
